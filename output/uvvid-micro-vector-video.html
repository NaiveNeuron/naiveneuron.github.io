uvvid - Micro Vector Video

Sat 22 October 2016

Uvvid is another project of ours. It tries to reduce size of educational videos in style of Khan Academy by translating strokes of a pen to vectors to save bandwidth. There are still many issues if you feel like landing a feel free to contact us at mrshu[at]neiveneuron.com, vidriduch[at]naiveneuron.com or by visiting freenode IRC channel #naiveneuron.
